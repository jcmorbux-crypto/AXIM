Developer Mode

Reveals raw technical detail in a few places for troubleshooting - internal trade/session IDs and raw execution error text in Trade History, and raw result data on Broker's test trades. Off by default: AXIM's day-to-day screens stay focused on your trading operation, not its internals. Mission Control never shows technical detail regardless of this toggle; connection/process diagnostics are always available in a collapsed "diagnostics" section on the Broker page.
